The Kuakata Cable Landing Station (Kuakata CLS) is located at Kuakata,
Patuakhali, Bangladesh, owned and operated by Bangladesh Submarine 
Cable Company (BSCCL) for the cable landing of SEA-WE-ME 5 (SMW5) 
in Bangladesh.

 Besides SMW5, SEA-WE-ME-4 also connects Bangladesh and lands at 
Cox's Bazar Cable Landing Station, owned and operated by BSCCL.
